Architect Workspace

Generate a shared OpenShift architecture pack with a live embedded draw.io preview, then export separate HLD or LLD documents with live cluster grounding and optional Red Hat or internal-document knowledge ingestion.

  • Pick a template or write a freeform OpenShift architecture brief.
  • Optionally collect live OpenShift state, train the pgvector knowledge base from URLs or uploaded files, and ask for clarification questions before generation.
  • Generate the shared architecture pack, preview the editable draw.io artifact directly in the browser, and export draw.io, SVG, PNG, and separate document-ready HLD or LLD content.
